# K1-Lightwave Landing Page Strategy

## Executive Summary

Pre-launch landing page for K1-Lightwave Founder's Edition: selling 100 units for market validation, beta testing, and early customer identification before full Kickstarter launch.

---

## Product Definition

**K1-Lightwave:** Dual edge-lit light guide plate (LGP) with 320 individually addressable RGB LEDs, powered by ESP32-S3. Form factor: 32cm light bar designed to sit under monitors on desks.

**Key Differentiator:** First-to-market dual edge-lit consumer device (not LED strip, not single-edge). Light enters from both sides for even diffusion with zero hotspots.

- Already use WLED, FastLED, custom controllers

- Form factor (under monitor) = exactly what they want

### Secondary (30% of messaging)

**3. Creative Lighting Enthusiasts**
- Streamers, photographers, content creators
- Need dynamic lighting for productions
- Music-reactive = competitive edge
- Will create demos/reviews

**4. Early Adopter Technophiles**
- "First 100 units" = catnip

### Who We're NOT Targeting

- Mass market consumers ("just want pretty lights")
- Non-technical users expecting perfection
- People needing extensive support
- Smart home normies (they'll come in v2)

## Pricing

**Single Tier: $249**

**Why one tier?**
- Simplicity (no decision paralysis)
- Equality (everyone gets same founder experience)
- Focus (entire page drives to ONE goal)

**Why $249?**
- High enough to signal premium (not toy)
- Low enough for impulse purchase (no budget approval)
- Comparable: Elgato Key Light ($199), Nanoleaf panels ($230)
- Covers COGS + margin for 100-unit run

**Payment Options:**
- Full: $249 now
- Split: 50% now ($125), 50% at ship ($124)
  - Reduces friction for fence-sitters
  - Locks in commitment

## Risk Mitigation

**What if we don't sell 100?**
- Threshold: 50 units minimum (break-even)
- If <50 in 60 days: Full refunds, pivot strategy
- If 50-99: Ship as planned, smaller beta cohort

**What if we oversell demand?**
- Strict cap at 100 units (no exceptions)
- Waitlist captures overflow â†’ Kickstarter pre-launch list
- Creates FOMO for v2 launch

**What if buyers are wrong audience?**
- Self-selection filter built into page ("This isn't for you if...")
- 30-day returns allow mismatch correction
- Discord engagement reveals true user needs

**K1 Advantages:**
- Dual edge-lit (unique)
- 320 individual LEDs (not zones)
- Designed form factor (not DIY)
- Open platform (not locked ecosystem)
